ASKY•

Skip the wait and ask away. Contact your healthcare provider wherever, whenever.

Let us help you organize
client inquiries.

The backbone of your client experience starts with the receptionist. Navigating client inquiries, coordinating follow-ups, and directing client questions to the right department have never been more seamless!

How it Works

1. Tell us about you

We collect the necessary information for your provider to follow up appropriately.

2 .Tell us your concern

Is it a post-op concern? Forgot how to take your medication? Something doesn't seem right? Describe it in detail.

3 .Your provider will respond

Your clinic will be in touch to follow up with your concern shorty!

ASKY is not for
medical emergencies.

Call your emergency provider if you are experiencing the following:

Chest pain


Sudden severe pain


Shortness of breath


Difficulty breathing


Bleeding that won't stop


Change in mental status


Fainting/loss of consciousness
